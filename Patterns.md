# Patterns

*Get design guidance for supporting common user actions, tasks, and experiences.*

- [Charting data](Patterns/Charting%20data.md) — Presenting data in a chart can help you communicate information with clarity and appeal.
- [Collaboration and sharing](Patterns/Collaboration%20and%20sharing.md) — Great collaboration and sharing experiences are simple and responsive, letting people engage with the content while communicating effectively with others.
- [Drag and drop](Patterns/Drag%20and%20drop.md) — Using drag and drop, people can move or duplicate selected photos, text, and other content by dragging the selection from one location to another.
- [Entering data](Patterns/Entering%20data.md) — When you need information from people, design ways that make it easy for them to provide it without making mistakes.
- [Feedback](Patterns/Feedback.md) — Feedback helps people know what’s happening, discover what they can do next, understand the results of actions, and avoid mistakes.
- [File management](Patterns/File%20management.md) — Some apps can support documents and files that people expect to manage throughout the system.
- [Going full screen](Patterns/Going%20full%20screen.md) — iPhone, iPad, and Mac offer full-screen modes that let people expand a window to fill the screen, hiding system controls and providing a distraction-free environment.
- [Launching](Patterns/Launching.md) — A streamlined launch experience helps people start using your app or game immediately.
- [Live-viewing apps](Patterns/Live-viewing%20apps.md) — As you design a live-viewing app, prioritize the content and create fun, fluid interactions that encourage immersion in the live-viewing experience.
- [Loading](Patterns/Loading.md) — The best content-loading experience finishes before people become aware of it.
- [Managing accounts](Patterns/Managing%20accounts.md) — When it doesn’t create an unnecessary barrier to your experience, an account can be a convenient way for people to access their content and track personal details.
- [Managing notifications](Patterns/Managing%20notifications.md) — Notifications can give people timely and important information, whether the device is locked or in use.
- [Modality](Patterns/Modality.md) — Modality is a design technique that presents content in a separate, dedicated mode that prevents interaction with the parent view and requires an explicit action to dismiss.
- [Multitasking](Patterns/Multitasking.md) — Multitasking lets people switch quickly from one app to another, performing tasks in each.
- [Offering help](Patterns/Offering%20help.md) — Although the most effective experiences are approachable and intuitive, you can provide contextual help when necessary.
- [Onboarding](Patterns/Onboarding.md) — Onboarding can help people get a quick start using your app or game.
- [Playing audio](Patterns/Playing%20audio.md) — People expect rich audio experiences that automatically adjust when the context changes on the device.
- [Playing haptics](Patterns/Playing%20haptics.md) — Playing haptics can engage people’s sense of touch and bring their familiarity with the physical world into your app or game.
- [Playing video](Patterns/Playing%20video.md) — People expect to enjoy rich video experiences on their devices, regardless of the app or game they’re using.
- [Printing](Patterns/Printing.md) — An iOS, iPadOS, macOS, or visionOS app can integrate system-provided print functionality when it makes sense, presenting custom printer- and document-specific options if necessary.
- [Ratings and reviews](Patterns/Ratings%20and%20reviews.md) — People often view the ratings and reviews for an app or game before they download it.
- [Searching](Patterns/Searching.md) — People use various search techniques to find content on their device, within an app, and within a document or file.
- [Settings](Patterns/Settings.md) — People expect apps and games to just work, but they also appreciate having ways to customize the experience to fit their needs.
- [Undo and redo](Patterns/Undo%20and%20redo.md) — Undo and redo gives people easy ways to reverse many types of actions, which can also help people explore and experiment safely as they learn a new interface or task.
- [Workouts](Patterns/Workouts.md) — A great workout or fitness experience encourages people to engage with their current activity and helps them track their progress on their devices.
