# Inputs

*Learn about the various methods people use to control your app or game and enter data.*

- [Action button](Inputs/Action%20button.md) — The Action button gives people quick access to their favorite features on supported iPhone and Apple Watch models.
- [Apple Pencil and Scribble](Inputs/Apple%20Pencil%20and%20Scribble.md) — Apple Pencil helps make drawing, handwriting, and marking effortless and natural, in addition to performing well as a pointer and UI interaction tool.
- [Camera Control](Inputs/Camera%20Control.md) — The Camera Control provides direct access to your app’s camera experience.
- [Digital Crown](Inputs/Digital%20Crown.md) — The Digital Crown is an important hardware input for Apple Vision Pro and Apple Watch.
- [Eyes](Inputs/Eyes.md) — In visionOS, people look at a virtual object to identify it as a target they can interact with.
- [Focus and selection](Inputs/Focus%20and%20selection.md) — Focus helps people visually confirm the object that their interaction targets.
- [Game controls](Inputs/Game%20controls.md) — Precise, intuitive game controls enhance gameplay and can increase a player’s immersion in the game.
- [Gestures](Inputs/Gestures.md) — A gesture is a physical motion that a person uses to directly affect an object in an app or game on their device.
- [Gyroscope and accelerometer](Inputs/Gyroscope%20and%20accelerometer.md) — On-device gyroscopes and accelerometers can supply data about a device’s movement in the physical world.
- [Keyboards](Inputs/Keyboards.md) — A physical keyboard can be an essential input device for entering text, playing games, controlling apps, and more.
- [Nearby interactions](Inputs/Nearby%20interactions.md) — Nearby interactions support on-device experiences that integrate the presence of people and objects in the nearby environment.
- [Pointing devices](Inputs/Pointing%20devices.md) — People can use a pointing device like a trackpad or mouse to navigate the interface and initiate actions.
- [Remotes](Inputs/Remotes.md) — The Siri Remote is the primary input method for Apple TV, helping people feel connected to onscreen content from across the room.
