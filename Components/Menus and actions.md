# Menus and actions

- [Activity views](Menus%20and%20actions/Activity%20views.md) — An activity view — often called a *share sheet* — presents a range of tasks that people can perform in the current context.
- [Buttons](Menus%20and%20actions/Buttons.md) — A button initiates an instantaneous action.
- [Context menus](Menus%20and%20actions/Context%20menus.md) — A context menu provides access to functionality that’s directly related to an item, without cluttering the interface.
- [Dock menus](Menus%20and%20actions/Dock%20menus.md) — On a Mac, people can secondary click an app’s or game’s icon in the Dock to reveal a Dock menu, which presents both system-provided and custom items.
- [Edit menus](Menus%20and%20actions/Edit%20menus.md) — An edit menu lets people make changes to selected content in the current view, in addition to offering related commands like Copy, Select, Translate, and Look Up.
- [Home Screen quick actions](Menus%20and%20actions/Home%20Screen%20quick%20actions.md) — Home Screen quick actions give people a way to perform app-specific actions from the Home Screen.
- [Menus](Menus%20and%20actions/Menus.md) — A menu reveals its options when people interact with it, making it a space-efficient way to present commands in your app or game.
- [Ornaments](Menus%20and%20actions/Ornaments.md) — In visionOS, an ornament presents controls and information related to a window, without crowding or obscuring the window’s contents.
- [Pop-up buttons](Menus%20and%20actions/Pop-up%20buttons.md) — A pop-up button displays a menu of mutually exclusive options.
- [Pull-down buttons](Menus%20and%20actions/Pull-down%20buttons.md) — A pull-down button displays a menu of items or actions that directly relate to the button’s purpose.
- [The menu bar](Menus%20and%20actions/The%20menu%20bar.md) — On a Mac or an iPad, the menu bar at the top of the screen displays the top-level menus in your app or game.
- [Toolbars](Menus%20and%20actions/Toolbars.md) — A toolbar provides convenient access to frequently used commands, controls, navigation, and search.
