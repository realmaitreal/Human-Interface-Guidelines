# Presentation

- [Action sheets](Presentation/Action%20sheets.md) — An action sheet is a modal view that presents choices related to an action people initiate.
- [Alerts](Presentation/Alerts.md) — An alert gives people critical information they need right away.
- [Page controls](Presentation/Page%20controls.md) — A page control displays a row of indicator images, each of which represents a page in a flat list.
- [Panels](Presentation/Panels.md) — In a macOS app, a panel typically floats above other open windows providing supplementary controls, options, or information related to the active window or current selection.
- [Popovers](Presentation/Popovers.md) — A popover is a transient view that appears above other content when people click or tap a control or interactive area.
- [Scroll views](Presentation/Scroll%20views.md) — A scroll view lets people view content that’s larger than the view’s boundaries by moving the content vertically or horizontally.
- [Sheets](Presentation/Sheets.md) — A sheet helps people perform a scoped task that’s closely related to their current context.
- [Windows](Presentation/Windows.md) — A window presents UI views and components in your app or game.
